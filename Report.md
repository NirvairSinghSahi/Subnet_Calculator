1. You must work in a team of 3 or 4 members for your project.
2. You have to demonstrate and present your final project in the Lab by the deadline.
3. A written final report has to be generated and submitted on the blackboard per group. Your
report must include the following parts:
Cover Page
Table of Contents
1. Introduction:
Introduce the objective and goals of the project.
2. Background and Literature Review
3. Design and Implementation:
Briefly describe how the project is implemented.
Design your topology or architecture based on your project. The topology/architecture
must include all devices used in your project. Make sure to label the devices. Use Visio
or equivalent application to design your topology or any figure used in your report.
Ensure that you use the appropriate icons / devices / media type(s) in your topology or
architecture diagram.
Description of the functional modules and functionalities.
Document all steps in your implementation, Coadding or Configurations based on your
project.
4. Results and Discussions:
Screenshots or images of results with discussions.
5. Conclusion:
Describe what have you learnt from the project. Mention some challenges and future
works on the project. Describe contributions of each member in your group clearly.
